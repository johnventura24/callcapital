FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is Call Capital?+

Call Capital is an operator-led membership ecosystem built on top of a real, operating call marketplace generating $2M+/month. It's three things at once: an education platform (learn how the call economy works from someone running it live), a community (operators, agents, media buyers, and aspiring brokers), and a participation platform (where members can plug into the ecosystem as publishers, agents, brokers, or buyers). It is not a course.

I'm an insurance agent — is this for me?+

Yes. If you're buying CPL leads and want to understand how inbound calls actually work — who generates them, how they're routed, and how to get direct access to quality supply — this will change how you operate. Inner Circle members can plug in as agents and close inbound calls directly, earning commission on every policy.

I have no experience in the call space. Can I still join?+

Yes. The free content and Core membership are specifically designed to take you from zero to operational. The playbook vault, weekly live sessions, and community will give you everything you need to understand the model and start building. You don't need experience — you need the willingness to execute.

What's the difference between Core and Inner Circle?+

Core ($149/mo) gets you inside the business — the full playbook vault, community, and the Deal Board. Inner Circle ($497/mo) gets you direct access: biweekly 60-minute live sessions with recordings and archive, access to our private group, co-build sessions where your campaigns get reviewed live, broker platform access to route calls through our marketplace, and agent seat access to close calls and earn commissions. Inner Circle is capped at 20 members.

What is the broker platform?+

Inner Circle members get access to our call distribution infrastructure — the same platform that routes 50,000+ calls per month. You can use this to build your own call brokering operation: source supply, route to buyers, and earn on the spread — without building the technology from scratch.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. No contracts. Cancel from your account anytime. If you're a founding member and cancel, you lose the locked rate — re-joining means paying current pricing. We offer a 30-day refund on your first month if you don't find value.
